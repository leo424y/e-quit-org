標題 【GLOBALink】世界新聞網：瑞士大眾運輸 禁電子菸
詳細資料

Use of e-cigarettes in public transport in Switzerland will be banned, the national association of mass transit operators said.

瑞士大眾運輸經營者協會表示，瑞士起將禁止在公共運輸系統上吸食電子菸。

use of the battery-powered devices that simulate1 smoking by heating and vaporising a liquid solution containing nicotine will incur2 a 25 Swiss franc fine, a spokesman for Public Transportation Union told the Swiss news agency ATS.

公共運輸聯盟的發言人告訴瑞士新聞社ATS說，吸食電子菸將會被處以25瑞士法郎罰金。電子菸是一種將含有尼古丁的溶液加熱與汽化，以模仿吸菸效果的電子裝置。

The regulation against smoking tobacco on public transport was extended to electronic cigarettes due to the difficulty of inspectors to distinguish3 between cigarettes and e-cigarettes.

由於檢查人員很難區別傳統香菸和電子菸的差異，所以決定把禁菸的規範擴大到電子菸。

Swiss regulations do not allow the sale of electronic cigarettes, but their use in the country is tolerated and the federal public health agency has noted an increase in their use.

瑞士法規不允許銷售電子菸，但不禁止吸食；瑞士聯邦公共衛生當局已發現電子菸日益普及。

There are currently no regulations in Switzerland governing their use in restaurants.

瑞士目前還沒有管理餐廳內吸食電子菸的規範。

While e-cigarettes contained some harmful substances, the levels of toxicants were nine to 450 times lower than in cigarette smoke.(AFP)

電子菸雖然含有一些有害物質，有害物質的濃度比傳統香菸少9到450倍。（法新社）
